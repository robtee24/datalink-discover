FAQ

Is Discover really free?

For approved, invited guests, there is no registration fee. This is not open registration—you apply first. For qualifying attendees, Datalink may also provide complimentary travel, lodging, and all scheduled meals and beverages tied to the official agenda. Your confirmation letter will spell out exactly what is covered for you.

What does “qualifying” mean?

We review each application for role, organization size and industry, strategic relevance to the topics, and cohort balance. Benefits such as travel and hotel are extended when your profile matches the program’s executive audience and capacity guidelines.

How are attendees selected?

Applications are reviewed based on leadership scope (for example, 300+ employee organizations are often a fit), decision-making authority, and alignment with AI, cloud, and cybersecurity themes. We keep each event intentionally small so every conversation earns its place in the room.
