Benny Bujiko

BarracudaPresenter — XDR

Benny Bujiko represents Barracuda on extended detection and response (XDR), a cybersecurity company delivering email protection, data security, and unified threat detection and response for organizations of all sizes.

His focus includes how security teams consolidate visibility across email, endpoints, networks, and cloud workloads; automate response; and reduce time to detect and remediate without uncontrolled tool sprawl. He speaks to both architectural choices and day-to-day SOC outcomes.
