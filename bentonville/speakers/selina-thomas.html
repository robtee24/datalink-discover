Selina Thomas

6 Degrees HR ConsultingCEO & Founder

Selina Thomas is CEO and Founder of 6 Degrees HR Consulting, a firm focused on human resources infrastructure, compliance, safety, and employee development for organizations that want human capital strategy aligned to business outcomes.

She brings 15+ years in HR, is PHR-certified (since 2008), and is known for careful implementation of HR systems, management support through education and professional associations, and hands-on work on critical projects including EDD and Labor Board matters. At Discover she represents the people-and-compliance lens alongside technical sessions on the same breakout rotation.
