Dr. Roméo Farinacci

Core InsightsPrincipal Consultant

Dr. Roméo Farinacci is Principal Consultant at Core Insights, a consultancy focused on cybersecurity governance, risk, and compliance for organizations that operate under strict regulatory and operational requirements.

With roughly two decades in GRC, cyber intelligence, audit, and incident and disaster-recovery planning, he connects security leadership, legal and regulatory expectations, and practical control design. His experience spans public, private, and government contexts and frameworks such as NIST, HIPAA, and PCI—making him a strong voice on risk prioritization and defensible programs.
