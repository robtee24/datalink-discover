TJ Turner

TD SYNNEXSolutions Architect, Cloud Engineering

TJ Turner is Solutions Architect in Cloud Engineering at TD SYNNEX, a global technology distributor and solutions aggregator that helps partners deliver complex infrastructure, hybrid, and cloud solutions to enterprise customers.

With more than 25 years across infrastructure and cloud architecture in sectors such as finance, education, SaaS, retail, and distribution, he translates business requirements into reference designs, migration approaches, and sustainable operations—especially around Azure, hybrid connectivity, and platform choices that balance performance, cost, and risk.

Away from the keyboard he is often on the golf course, in pickleball matches with his kids, or cooking something worth sitting down to—details that show up on Datalink’s public speaker materials alongside his technical track record.
