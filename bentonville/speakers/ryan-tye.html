Ryan Tye

TD SYNNEXBusiness Development Account Manager, Microsoft Azure

Ryan Tye is Business Development Account Manager for Microsoft Azure at TD SYNNEX, a global technology distributor and solutions aggregator that helps partners design, sell, and support Microsoft cloud and multivendor solutions at scale.

He has spent six years on TD SYNNEX’s Azure team supporting top partners across the central and western U.S. as a primary pre-sales contact—driving new Microsoft initiatives with emphasis on Azure cost savings and navigating evolving billing models. His work spans estate strategy, capacity planning, and practical conversations about getting more value from current and planned Azure investments.
