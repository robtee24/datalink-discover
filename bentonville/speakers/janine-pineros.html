Janine Pineros

KnowBe4Security awareness & human risk

Janine Pineros represents KnowBe4, a leader in security awareness training, simulated phishing, and managing human risk as part of a broader security and compliance program.

She is well suited to discuss how organizations build security culture, measure behavior change, and tie awareness outcomes to phishing risk, insider threats, and regulatory expectations. Her perspective complements technical controls by addressing the human layer executives increasingly weigh in board-level reporting.
