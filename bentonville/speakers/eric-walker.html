Eric Walker

HPWindows / endpoint ecosystem

Eric Walker represents HP for the Windows and commercial PC ecosystem, part of a global technology company delivering business PCs, peripherals, and services that underpin modern workforce productivity.

In this capacity he is versed in enterprise device fleets, Windows security baselines, manageability at scale, and how endpoints integrate with identity, patch, and zero-trust programs. His breakout contributions center on practical decisions IT leaders make when standardizing, securing, and supporting PCs across distributed organizations.
