Don Wisdom

Datalink NetworksPresident

Don Wisdom is President of Datalink Networks, a national IT solutions and services provider partnering with enterprises on Microsoft cloud, security, and modern workplace outcomes.

As president, he sets strategic direction for Datalink’s portfolio—managed IT and security services, Microsoft cloud and licensing, consulting, and solutions design—and works with enterprise clients and strategic vendors on long-term technology roadmaps. He opens Discover with remarks that frame how AI, cloud platforms, and cybersecurity priorities fit together for senior IT and security leaders.
