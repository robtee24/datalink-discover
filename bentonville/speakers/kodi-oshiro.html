Kodi Oshiro

Datalink NetworksSOC Analyst I

Kodi Oshiro is SOC Analyst I at Datalink Networks, a national IT solutions and services provider partnering with enterprises on Microsoft cloud, security, and modern workplace outcomes.

He supports Datalink Networks' SOC with day-to-day monitoring, alert validation, and collaboration on investigations—building familiarity with detection logic, log sources, and escalation paths. Together with Luke Waltz, he offers a grounded perspective on what modern SOC work looks like for teams defending mid-market and enterprise environments.
