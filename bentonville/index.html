Why we gate attendance

Discover works because every seat is intentional. We approve attendees in advance so discussions stay strategic—not promotional. That scarcity is a feature: you are in the room with people who share your remit and decision authority.

  • Approval before the event — you will know if you are confirmed (or waitlisted) before you book travel.
  • Expenses covered for qualifying guests — many approved leaders receive air or ground travel, hotel, and all hosted meals and receptions.
  • No registration fee — for invited, approved attendees, there is no ticket price.
