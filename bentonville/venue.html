About The Ledger

At roughly 230,000 sq. ft., The Ledger is designed as a downtown hub for work and community — private and shared workplaces, reservable meeting and event space, retail, art, and the signature exterior bike ramps. Ramps are open to the public during business hours; members access additional bike-friendly amenities.

Lodging & what “all expenses covered” can include

For qualifying invited guests, hotel accommodations for the official program are typically provided at no cost as part of your complimentary package. Per Datalink’s public FAQ, all expenses covered may include roughly two nights of hotel, scheduled meals and dining, local transportation in Bentonville, conference materials, and networking events — while travel to and from Bentonville is often your responsibility unless your confirmation states otherwise. Exact inclusions are always confirmed in writing after review.

Getting here

  • Air: Northwest Arkansas National Airport (XNA) is the primary airport. If you are approved for a travel-inclusive package, booking instructions and ground coordination are provided in your confirmation—not on this marketing page.
  • Driving: Bentonville is accessible via I-49 and regional highways. Parking details are shared with confirmed guests.
